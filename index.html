Hello, we’re Curfew Grooming. A Brixton-based barbershop,
launched in 2020.

We were born in a year that no one will ever want to remember,
with a name that can’t be forgotten.

Just like our name, and just like our advertising, we’re ready to ruffle a few feathers.

As people we are honest, opinionated, and a little rebellious.

And like you, we believe there are lots of things
that need to change right now.

We can’t wait to meet you.
